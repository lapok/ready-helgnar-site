ABOUT THE NEW
VIDEOGAME

Helgnar the Conqueror is a story-driven, third-person role-playing action/RPG game set in the extraordinary Ruthless Warworld. In the single-player adventure video game, players can expect a detailed, multi-layered, thrilling story with long-lasting double consequences and diverse, dynamic gameplay: bloody throne wars, epically staged conquests of kingdoms, hunting genetically modified monsters, and detective investigations.

The toughest choices in your life and a revolutionary new role-playing Realistic Connections mechanics will evoke a full range of emotions and give you unforgettable moments throughout the video game, truly bringing to life the complicated characters and strengthening your relationships with them to an indescribable level.

Hellknights Studios offers something completely new to the industry ‚Äî the Personality Formation mechanic. Throughout the game, you make difficult, ambiguous moral decisions that easily influence the characters traveling alongside you. Each of the 7 key profound characters goes through their own paths of formation, changing in front of your eyes and it is only up to you to determine who they will become at the end of their brutal path.

Due to the Fate Maker mechanics, from now on the lives of absolutely all characters are only at your fingertips. None of the characters are immune, even the key figures, each of the key characters can die and will die because of your ambiguous actions. Want to fulfill your Doomsday destiny and burn down the Ruthless World of War so that new innocent life can be born from the ashes? Then burn the doomed Warworld to the ground! From now on, everything depends only on you and your ambiguous choices throughout the game. Throughout the emotional and brutal story, you'll shape many fates, save, and kill many ambiguous characters. Depending on your actions, each of these complex personalities may save or kill your most favorite character in the future. You will determine the destinies of all the characters based on your own beliefs and values.

Fatal Choices will make you nervous and emotional with every choice of dialog option, because behind every choice you can expect Fatal Death. With every choice, you will absolutely feel emotions, because every choice you make kills someone, changes destinies or shapes personalities. In Warworld, you are always responsible for your words and actions.

The Realistic Relationship mechanics will give you the unforgettable emotions of a complex romantic relationship and will truly vitalize your partner. To win the hearts of key female characters, you have a long way to go ‚Äì to hear and listen to living personalities with their own opinions. Actually, during your travels, the companion has been analyzing you based on your ambiguous choices, and will eventually give a verdict on all your actions. It is not you who chooses who to be with, but the characters who come to life in your sight decide if you are worthy of them. The Personality Formation mechanics has merely deepened key romantic lines and strengthened the player's bonds with the characters to an indescribable level.

The revolutionary Realistic Connections mechanics creates incredible bonds between the player and the characters that come to life in front of his eyes, blur the lines between the game and reality.
